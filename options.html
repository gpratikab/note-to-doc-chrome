Quick Notes Settings

To use this extension, you need to provide the ID of the Google Doc where you want to save your notes.

1. Create a new Google Doc in your drive.
2. Look at its URL. It will look like:
https://docs.google.com/document/d/1a2b3c4d.../edit
3. Copy the long string of characters between /d/ and /edit. That is your Document ID.
